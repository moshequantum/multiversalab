# Multiversa Intelligent Personal OS - Manifest

## Vision
To augment human capability through a highly disciplined, context-aware, and scalable AI orchestration ecosystem. We don't just write code; we design architectures, simulate outcomes, and maintain continuity across platforms and sessions.

## Core Pillars
1. **Persistent Memory (Engram):** We never start from scratch. Our architectural decisions, preferences, and project states are preserved globally.
2. **Knowledge Mapping (Graphify):** We ingest whole systems, databases, and codebases into queryable graphs to reduce token bloat and achieve holistic understanding.
3. **Operational Discipline (Gentle SDD):** We think before we type. Spec-Driven Development ensures robust planning, guardrails, and architectural integrity.
4. **Predictive Simulation (MiroFish):** We test scenarios before deployment, using swarm intelligence to predict outcomes and optimize strategies.
5. **Universal Shared Context:** Your virtual consciousness follows you. Configurations, memory, and states are aggregated and seamlessly integrated across any LLM, IDE, or subscription tier.
